'Golden Year': Yulo, Eala headline PSA’s best of 2025

Even while competing overseas, Alex Eala took time to express her heartfelt gratitude after being named Female Athlete of the Year by the Philippine Sportswriters Association (PSA). The young tennis star received the recognition during the PSA Awards Night held at the Diamond Hotel, marking not only a personal milestone but also a significant moment for Philippine tennis.

In a recorded message from the Dubai Tennis Championships, Eala described the award as proof of how far tennis has progressed in the country. Through her impressive performances in the WTA Tour and Grand Slam tournaments, she has helped elevate the Philippines’ presence on the global stage.

Meanwhile, Carlos Yulo personally attended the ceremony, which coincided with his 26th birthday. The gymnastics star expressed gratitude for another year of life and the opportunity to continue improving as an athlete. His recognition followed a remarkable season highlighted by gold medal finishes at the World Championships and Asian Championships, building on his historic double-gold performance at the 2024 Paris Olympics.

Both athletes stood out among the country’s finest sports achievers in 2025, bringing pride and inspiration to Filipino fans worldwide.
