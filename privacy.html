Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE DATE: FEBRUARY 2, 2026

UrbanPulse ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our readers. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or make an account at UrbanPulse.

1. Information We Collect

When you enroll, we collect specific "Account Data," which includes your full name and email address. We also collect "Technical Data," such as:

  • IP addresses and browser user-agent strings for security and fraud prevention.
  • Time zone settings and location data to provide localized news reports.
  • Usage data, including which articles you interact with most frequently.

2. How We Use Your Data

We process your data to fulfill our contract with you (providing news services) and for our legitimate business interests, including:

  • Personalization: Tailoring the "Ripple" news feed to your interests.
  • Communication: Sending daily briefings and breaking news alerts.
  • Security: Monitoring for malicious login attempts and protecting our community.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregated demographic data with our advertising partners. We may disclose your data if required by law or to protect the safety of our journalists and staff.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your Account Data for as long as your account is active. If you choose to delete your account, your personal data will be purged from our active databases within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal or audit purposes.

5. Your Legal Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in Europe or CCPA in California), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise these rights, please email our Data Protection Officer at privacy@urbanpulse.com.
